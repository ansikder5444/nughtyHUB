    A proper latch is essential to prevent nipple pain and ensure your baby is getting enough milk. Make sure your baby’s mouth covers not only the nipple but also a good portion of the areola.

    Sleep can be limited with a newborn, so try to rest or nap when your baby does. This helps with your overall energy and milk production.
